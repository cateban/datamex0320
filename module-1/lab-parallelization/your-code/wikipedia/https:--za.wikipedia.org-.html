Vangz Yenfanh (黃現璠, 1899 nienz 11 nyied 13 hauh — 1982 nienz 1 nyied 18 hauh), Cunghgoz lisij yozgyah、minzcuz yozgyah、gyauyuzgyah、sevei hozdunggyah. Mingzcoh nyuenz heuh Ganh Ginjyingh (甘錦英), dwg vunz Gvangjsih Fuzsuih Yen Gizgiu Cin Mbanj Gizswh, Bouxcuengh. Vangz Yenfanh gij cungyau gungyen gwnz yozsuz biujyienh youq de boux daih’it haicaux le fungheiq ndei Cuenghhag yenzgiu,cauxlaeb le gwnz lizsij minzcuz Cungguek aen saujsu minzcuz yozbaij daih’it—Bazgveiq yozbaij(Betgveiq yozbaij). Doengzseiz de lij gamj yiengq gyaudiuzcujyi sijyoz diuhcan, daez ok le gij yawjfap moq Cungguek lizsij mboujmiz nuzli sevei, baenzneix ndaej yozsuzgai goeng nyinh guh bouxdaeuz Bazgveiq yozbaij caeuq daujswh Fouznuz yozbaij.
