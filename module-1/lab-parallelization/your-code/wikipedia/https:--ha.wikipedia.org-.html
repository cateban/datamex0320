Muhimmiyar Sanarwa

Barka da zuwa Hausa Wikipedia, Ɗaya daga cikin shafukan Wikipedia a Harshen Hausa, zaku iya rubutawa da ingantawa ko kuma ƙirƙiran sabbin muƙaloli kamar yadda kuke gani, dan taimako wurin rubuta kundin Ilimi na Insakulofidiya wadda ke taskance ilimi dan masu karatu da yin bincike a harshen Hausa.
Ku karanta shafin mu na Gabatarwa. Idan kuna neman wani taimako, zaku iya tamabaya anan. Sannan kuna iya bin mu a shafukan sada zumunta: @WikipediaHausa akan (Twitter) ko a Wikipedia da harshen Hausa akan (Facebook).

Wasu muƙaloli da ake bukata

  • Mutane: Rilwanu Adamu Jumba, Jacob Gyang Buba, Ibrahim Baré Maïnassara, Abdulkareem Baba Aminu, Sani Kaita, Moussa Maâzou, Amadou Moutari, Umaru Mutallab, Askia Muhammad, Heinrich Barth, Alhassan Yusuf
  • Filayen jirgin sama: Filin jirgin saman Abidjan, Filin jirgin saman Dakar, Filin jirgin saman Accra, Filin jirgin saman Douala
  • Abinci: Kosai, Amala, Funkaso

  • Indonesia, Asiya, Iran, Najeriya, Saudiyya, Amurka, Jamus, Indiya, Sudan, Masar, Kenya, Ghana, Togo, Nijar, Turkiyya, Iraki, Pakistan, Kuwait, Lebanon, Siriya, Jordan, Zimbabwe, Burkina Faso, Angola, Aljeriya, Somaliya, Senegal, Zambiya, Mali
