Aaj ke featured article

Main ek 1,691 ton, loha ke jahaaj rahaa jiske India se Fiji girmitya log ke laae khatir kaam me lawa gais rahaa. Ii jahaaj ke 1864 me Russel and Company, Nourse Line khatir banais rahaa. 30 April 1885 ke ii jahaaj 735 girmitya log ke lae ke Suva pahuchis. Iske baad ii jahaaj ke West Indies girmitya log ke lae jae ke khatir kamm me lawa gais. 1910 me iske Norway ke ek Company ke bech dewa gae aur iske naam badal ke Vansa rakha gais. (aur...)


Aap janta hai...

Ratu Cakobau
  • 1875 ke measles epidemic, jisme 20% Kaiviti logan mar gae rahin, ke Fiji me failae waala, Ratu Cakobau aur uske dui larrka logan rahin jon ki March 1875 me Sydney se ghuum ke lauta rahin.
  • 1879 se lae ke 1916 tak, 42 jahaaj, 87 trip me 60,553 girmitya logan ke lae ke Fiji se aais rahaa. Isme se 45,439 log Calcutta se aur 15,114 log Madras se jahaaj me charrhin rahaa.
  • Fiji ke Legislative Council ke pahila Hindustani member Badri Maharaj rahaa, jiske Governor nominate karis rahaa aur jon 1917 - 1923 aur 1926 - 1929 tak Council ke member rahaa.
  • Fiji me pahila football competition Nasau Park, Levuka me 1924 me, Cession ke golden jublee celebrate kare ke time, chhe Kaiviti team ke biich me Ricarnie Cup khatir, khela gais rahaa.
  • Fiji ke Hindustani ke army me join kare ke khatir, 1934 me Indian Platoon banawa gais rahaa lekin iske 1940 me disband kar dewa gais rahaa.
  • Royal Air Force ke khatir fighter plane kharide ke khatir, 1943 me ek Fighter Fund suruu karaa gais rahaa, aur ii paisa se nau fighter aur paanch bomber kharida gais rahaa. Ek fighter ke naam Fiji Indian Fighter rahaa.
  • Fiji Trade Union Congress se pahile Ami Chandra, 1950s me Fiji Industrial Workers Congress suruu karis rahaa.
  • Fiji ke 1997 ke constitution me English aur Kaiviti ke saathe Hindustani ke Fiji ke official language banawa gais rahaa.

Taaja khabar

  • 11 August - Lautoka ke Churchill Park me Labasa, Lautoka ke 1 -0 se harae ke 2019 Battle Of Giants, ke jiit liis.
  • 29 June - Savusavu me SODELPA ke annual general meeting me, ek barraa kaiviti ratu, ii bolis ki NFP aur FLP 'vulagi' (mehmaan) ke party hai.
  • 27 June - Fiji Trades Union Congress ke general secretary, Felix Anthony ke employee contract ke baare me jhuuta baat bole ke aarop me giraftaar karaa gais .
  • 12 May - Lautoka ke Churchill Park me Nadi, Suva ke 2 -0 se harae ke 2019 Fiji FACT ke jiit liis.
  • 26 December - Fiji me paida bhae, California ke polise, Ronil Singh ke ek driver jiske uu rokais rahaa, bandook se daag ke aar diis.
  • 11 July - Fiji ke jaana maana sikchhak, Dr Saukat Ali Sahib ke maut hoe gais. Uu Fiji me parrhai likhai ke khaatir bahut kaam karis rahaa aur dui school bhi suruu karis rahaa.
2017 ke Archive

Aaj se sau saal pahile, 1917 me...

  • Ek newspaper, Indian Settler, ke Fiji me suruu karaa gais rahaa, jiske English hissa ke sdapaadak, Manilal Doctor, aur Hindi hissa ke sampaadak Swami Manoharanand rahaa.
  • 9 January - Allahabad, India me girmit ke birod me jaluus nikaala gais, aur isme Motilal Nehru, H.S.L. Polak aur Sarojini Naidu bhasan diin.
  • 29 January - Badri Maharaj, jon ki Fiji ke Legislative Council ke pahila Indian nominated member rahaa, Council me aapan siit liis.
  • 12 March - Defence of India Act ke niche, India ke sarkar, British colony ke khaatir majuur ke recruit kare ke band kar diis, larrai ke band hoe tak, nai to dui saal talak.
  • June - C. F. Andrews, June se November talak Fiji me rahaa, aur ii paais ki Hindustani majuur mahangaai se dukh me rahin aur gimitya logan ke talab girmit suruu hoe se barrhaaya nai gais hai. Uu Fiji me dui school bhi banais.
  • 19 November - Indira Gandhi, jon ki India ke Prime Minister chaar term tak rahis, ke janam bhais rahaa.
  • 1916 me na koi girmitya logan Fiji aain, aur jahaaj nai mile ke kaaran, na koi logan India lautin.
